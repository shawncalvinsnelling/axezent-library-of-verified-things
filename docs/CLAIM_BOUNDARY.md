# Claim Boundary

Axezent AI — Library of Verified Things is a finite receipt-verification business layer.

## Accepted public claim

> Given a submitted finite receipt and declared local checks, the verifier reports whether the receipt satisfies those deterministic checks.

## Rejected overclaims

The project does not claim to prove:

- global AI truthfulness;
- full AI alignment;
- hidden chain-of-thought or hidden model internals;
- all future model outputs;
- correctness of an external solver;
- a complete proof of RH, P vs NP, or any Millennium Prize problem;
- financial, legal, medical, or investment results.

## Required phrase

```text
No claim without a receipt.
```

## Public label

```text
PUBLIC_BUSINESS_LAYER__FINITE_RECEIPT_VERIFICATION__NO_GLOBAL_PROOF_CLAIM
```
