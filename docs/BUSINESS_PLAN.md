# Axezent AI — Library of Verified Things Business Plan

## Company concept

Axezent AI turns claims into receipt-backed verification records. The first product is a simple public website and verifier that lets a user paste a JSON receipt and receive an audit-ready result.

## Customer segments

1. AI-agent builders who need output-trace compliance receipts.
2. Open-source maintainers who want reproducible release certificates.
3. Research teams that need artifact ledgers for code, data, and proof claims.
4. Enterprise teams that need private dashboards and custom policy packs.
5. Education programs teaching formal verification, AI safety, and reproducible computing.

## Product tiers

### Open-Core — Free

- Static website
- Browser verifier
- CLI verifier
- Sample receipt templates
- Public claim-boundary docs

### Founder Pilot — $500 to $2,500

- Custom receipt schema
- Customer onboarding call
- Private starter policy pack
- Signed certificate template
- Launch dashboard customization

### Enterprise — Custom

- Hosted private receipt vault
- Enterprise dashboard
- Custom policy packs
- High-throughput integrations
- Team training
- Long-term receipt storage
- Security review workflow

## Initial offer

"Send us one AI-agent workflow, software release, or research artifact. We convert it into a finite receipt and give you a replayable verification certificate."

## Revenue paths

- setup fees;
- monthly dashboard subscription;
- certificate generation fees;
- private policy-pack development;
- enterprise support;
- training workshops;
- hosted receipt vaults.

## Operating principle

The business wins by being more honest than competitors: it verifies exactly what the receipt proves, and refuses anything outside the scope.
